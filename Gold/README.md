## ğŸ“ Vault Structure
The AI Employee operates within a structured Obsidian vault:
- `/Inbox`: Raw data drops.
- `/Needs_Action`: Tasks detected by watchers requiring processing.
- `/Plans`: Multi-step execution plans generated by the reasoning engine.
- `/Pending_Approval`: Sensitive actions (Payments, Social) waiting for you.
- `/Approved`: Actions you have greenlit.
- `/Done`: Historical record of all completed work.
- `/Accounting`: Financial ledgers and Odoo audit logs.
- `/Briefings`: Weekly Monday Morning CEO Reports.
- `/Logs`: Enterprise-grade daily audit logs (90-day retention).
- `/Quarantine`: Corrupted or suspicious files isolated for safety.
